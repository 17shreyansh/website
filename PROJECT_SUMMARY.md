# RSD Bharti Industries Website - Project Summary

## 🎯 Project Overview

A premium, modern website for RSD Bharti Industries Pvt. Ltd., a construction materials company with 25+ years of experience. The website promotes their mobile app and establishes brand authority in the construction industry.

## ✨ Key Features Delivered

### Design & Branding
- ✅ Premium royal purple & gold color scheme
- ✅ Corporate, luxury aesthetic
- ✅ Apple-style clean layouts
- ✅ Professional typography (Inter font)
- ✅ Elegant whitespace and spacing
- ✅ No cheap gradients or neon colors

### Technical Implementation
- ✅ Next.js 14 (App Router)
- ✅ React 18 with TypeScript
- ✅ Tailwind CSS for styling
- ✅ Framer Motion for animations
- ✅ Lucide React icons
- ✅ Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- ✅ Component-based architecture
- ✅ SEO optimized
- ✅ Performance optimized

### Pages Implemented

#### 1. Home Page (/)
- Hero section with compelling tagline
- Trust statistics (25+ years, 12+ brands, 1000+ projects)
- About preview with 3 premium cards
- Materials showcase (Cement, Steel, Essentials)
- Mobile app promotion section
- Multiple CTAs
- Smooth scroll animations

#### 2. About Page (/about)
- Company story and history
- Mission & Vision statements
- 25-year timeline with milestones
- Core values section
- Statistics showcase
- Professional narrative

#### 3. Materials Page (/materials)
- Premium Cement section (6 brands)
  - UltraTech, Dalmia, ACC, Ambuja, Shree, Birla
- TMT Steel section (5 brands)
  - Tata Tiscon, JSW, Jindal, Shyam, Kamdhenu
- Construction Essentials (6 categories)
  - Sand, Stone Chips, Binding Wire, etc.
- Quality assurance badges
- Clean grid layouts

#### 4. Contact Page (/contact)
- Professional contact form
- Contact information cards
- Phone, email, address details
- Business hours
- Map placeholder
- Form validation

### Typography
- Font: Inter (Google Fonts)
- Headings: Bold, tight tracking
- Body: Regular, relaxed leading
- Sizes: Responsive (mobile to desktop)

### Spacing
- Consistent padding/margin
- Section spacing: 24 (py-24)
- Card padding: 8 (p-8)
- Grid gaps: 8 (gap-8)

- Subtle fade-in effects
- Smooth hover transitions
